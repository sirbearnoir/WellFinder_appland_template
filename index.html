About WellFinder

According to the Centers for Disease Control, 6 in 10 Americans live with at least one chronic health condition. The CDC also estimates that 90% of the $4.1 trillion spent each year on healthcare costs are for people with chronic health conditions.

Fortunately, making some simple changes can improve daily life for many people living with chronic health conditions, and can lower health expenditures too.

That’s where WellFinder comes in. WellFinder provides a simple, tested strategy for building healthy habits and noticing the connections between physical, emotional, and social well-being, so that every person, wherever they are starting from, can find their own “well”.

Manage Nutrition

Whether you need to track a new diet or keep tabs on an old dietary trigger WellFinder can help you dine well.

Track Symptoms

WellFinder is the painfree way to daily track your physical and emotion condition.

Tackle Activity

If you need to know how your day in and day out activity effects your symptoms WellFinder can help you spot a trend.

Connect Securely

WellFinder offers a safe and secure way to collect health data to share with medical professionals.

Receive Feedback

Inspire Mindfulness

Wellness is a lifelong activity. WellFinder can help keep you feeling more like yourself on your journey.
