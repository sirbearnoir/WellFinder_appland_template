About WellFinder

According to the Centers for Disease Control, 6 in 10 Americans live with at least one chronic health condition. The CDC also estimates that 90% of the $4.1 trillion spent each year on healthcare costs are for people with chronic health conditions.


Fortunately, making some simple changes can improve daily life for many people living with chronic health conditions, and can lower health expenditures too. That’s where WellFinder comes in. WellFinder provides a simple, tested strategy for building healthy habits and noticing the connections between physical, emotional, and social well-being, so that every person, wherever they are starting from, can find their own “well”.

Manage Nutrition

Whether you want to improve your diet or keep tabs on an old dietary trigger WellFinder can help you dine well.

Track Symptoms and Medications

WellFinder is the painfree way to track your physical and emotional condition every day.

Get Moving

Set and track goals for adding more movement into your life, or track how your daily activities affect your symptoms.

Receive Reminders

Habits only build when we do them. WellFinder's notifications make it easier to follow through on your health-related goals, on your schedule.

Get Ideas and Receive Feedback

We all need a little help sometimes. Use our quizzes to set goals, and let our expert-generated suggestions ease your burden.

Stay the Course

Wellness is a lifelong activity, and it looks a little different for everyone. WellFinder can help keep you feeling more like yourself on your journey.
